Who I am?

About Me

I am a software developer based in Aachen.

I love to develop new and modern software to help and improve the lives of people that uses the software. In today’s world, we have so much to do and to think about that is can be very useful to automate or simplify some routines or processes to have more time for others.

Journey

The crucial realization came to me after my school days, when it became clear to me where my passion truly leads. I chose software development. However, it was only after completing my education that I became aware that this was just the beginning and a long journey still lay ahead of me – a journey where learning never ends. During this exciting journey, one project fascinated me in particular: Excellus. The opportunity to ease the work of others through my project and to see it actually being used has evoked a deep sense of excitement within me.
